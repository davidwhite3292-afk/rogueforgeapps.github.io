Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

RogueForge Apps, LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built FlintFocus as a productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FlintFocus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data — including timer settings, focus sessions, streaks, and statistics — is stored locally on your device only.

No Account Required

FlintFocus does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Analytics and Tracking

FlintFocus does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, location, or any other personal data.

In-App Purchases

FlintFocus offers optional in-app purchases (subscriptions and a lifetime option) through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

FlintFocus does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not contain any objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rogueforgeapps.com.
