Frequently Asked Questions

Does the timer keep running when I switch apps?

Yes! FlintFocus is designed to keep counting down even when you switch to another app or lock your phone. You'll get a notification when your session ends.

Will FlintFocus stop my music?

No. FlintFocus is completely music-friendly. Your music, podcasts, and audiobooks will keep playing while you focus.

How do I change the timer duration?

Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner of the main screen to open Settings. From there you can adjust your focus time, short break, and long break durations.

What does the Pro upgrade include?

FlintFocus Pro unlocks weekly and monthly stats, streak achievements, home screen widgets, and unlimited timer presets. You can upgrade from Settings.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, tap "Upgrade", and then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > FlintFocus to manage or cancel.
