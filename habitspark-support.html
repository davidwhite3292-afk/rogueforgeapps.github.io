Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the green + button at the bottom right of the main screen. Give your habit a name, pick an icon and color, and optionally set a daily reminder.

How do I edit or delete a habit?

Tap on any habit in your list to open the edit screen. From there you can change the name, icon, color, or reminder. You can also delete the habit at the bottom of that screen.

My notifications aren't working — what should I do?

Make sure notifications are enabled for HabitSpark in your iPhone Settings. Go to Settings > Notifications > HabitSpark and make sure "Allow Notifications" is turned on.

How do streaks work?

A streak counts how many days in a row you've completed a habit. If you miss a day, the streak resets to zero. You can see your current streak and longest streak in the calendar view or the insights screen.

What does HabitSpark Pro include?

Pro unlocks unlimited habits (free is limited to 10), a home screen widget, and detailed insights. It's a one-time purchase of $9.99 — no subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap the gear icon to go to Settings, tap "Upgrade to Pro", and then tap "Restore Purchase" at the bottom of the screen.

Can I check off habits for past days?

Yes! Tap the calendar icon in the top right corner. In the week view, you can tap on any past day's circle to mark it as completed or uncompleted.
